Privacy Policy

Effective July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Caliza Group LLC (“Julian,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), which operates reachjulian.com (the “Site”), handles information from business owners who contact us about a possible sale. Your confidentiality is central to how we work.

The short version

We never sell, rent, or trade your information. We don’t list your business or tell anyone you’re thinking of selling. Once we sign a mutual NDA, we’re both legally bound to keep things confidential. And we’ll permanently delete everything we hold about you whenever you ask.

01

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you use our contact form or communicate with us, we collect what you choose to share — typically your name, email, phone number (optional), city or area, type of business, an approximate annual revenue range, and anything you write in your message. Later in the process you may share additional business or financial details.

Information collected automatically. We keep the Site deliberately lightweight. Our hosting and form providers may record standard technical data (such as IP address and browser type) for security and delivery. The Site also loads fonts from a third-party provider, which may receive your IP address. We do not run advertising trackers.

We collect only what we reasonably need to evaluate a possible transaction.

02

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to you, evaluate a potential acquisition, communicate about it, prepare and carry out a mutual NDA and any resulting transaction, keep appropriate records, and comply with the law. We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not build or sell marketing lists.

03

Confidential before any NDA

Even before any agreement is signed, we treat the fact that you contacted us — and everything you share — as confidential. We do not publicly list your business, disclose that it may be for sale, or share your identity or details for any purpose unrelated to evaluating a possible purchase.

04

The mutual NDA

If we move forward, we sign a mutual non-disclosure agreement. From that point, we are contractually bound to protect your information under the terms of that NDA — and so are you. If anything in this Policy differs from a signed NDA, the NDA controls for the information it covers.

05

When we may share information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We may share limited information only in these situations:

  • With our own professional advisors (such as attorneys, accountants, or financing sources) who need it to evaluate or complete a transaction and are bound by confidentiality;
  • With service providers who help us operate the Site or receive form submissions, under obligations to protect it; and
  • Where required by law, legal process, or to establish or defend legal rights.

That’s the full list.

06

Keeping — and destroying — your data

We keep your information only as long as needed to evaluate or pursue a possible transaction, or as the law requires. You can ask us to delete everything we hold about you at any time, and we will permanently destroy it promptly — other than any limited records we are legally required to retain. To request deletion, email julian@reachjulian.com.

07

How we protect your information

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08

Your choices and rights

You may decline to provide information (though we may then be unable to evaluate a purchase), ask to see or correct what we hold, or ask us to delete it. Depending on where you live — including under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act — you may have additional rights. Contact us and we will respond as applicable law requires.

09

Third-party links

The Site or our messages may link to websites we do not control and are not responsible for. Their privacy practices are their own; please review them separately.

10

Not for children

The Site is intended for business owners and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Effective” date above shows the latest version, and material changes take effect when posted here.

12

Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy: julian@reachjulian.com.
