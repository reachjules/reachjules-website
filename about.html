My background

I’m an operator at heart. Over 18 years as an entrepreneur, I’ve spent the last dozen deep in home goods and health & wellness — categories that live and die on repeat customers and trust. I learned the parts of a business that actually move the needle: tight operations and marketing that earns its keep.

That’s the lens I bring to every business I look at. Not “where can we cut,” but “where is this already working, and how do we help it run better.”

Why I buy businesses like yours

Plenty of buyers are financial engineers — they buy to strip a business down and move on. I’m the opposite. I buy businesses to own and run them, and I grow them the way I always have: by sharpening operations and making the marketing more efficient, not by gutting the team that made the place work.

If you built something with loyal, repeat customers, that’s exactly what I’m looking for — and exactly what I want to protect.

Why Texas

I’ve lived in Texas for 20 years, and my wife is a sixth-generation Texan. This is home. I’d rather see a good Texas business stay in Texan hands than get absorbed by a fund three states away that’s never set foot in your town.

What I’m like to work with

Direct, honest, and confidential. You deal with me — not a pipeline of associates. I’ll tell you straight whether we’re a fit, I’ll move at a pace that respects your life’s work, and nothing you share ever gets listed or passed around.

Let’s have a quiet conversation.

If you’re thinking about selling — even if you’re just starting to — reach out. No pressure, no obligation.
